How do I sign up?

Registration starts Sunday November 25th. Pick up a flyer and registration form in your parish, fill it out and send it along with your check (made out to B4J) to address provided in the form.


How much?

Cost is $355 per first child, $350 per subsequent child from the same family (brother or sister). After December 31st 2012, the cost becomes $425 per first child and $420 for second child. Cheques must be dated no later than December 31st 2012.


Note

Registration is on a first come first serve basis. We must have your registration form and cheque before your spot can be reserved (no faxes please). You will be notified when we receive your application.
Space is very limited (60 participants), do not delay!
